Dr. Sunbin Deng received his B.S. degree in Optical information science and technology from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), Wuhan, China in 2014 and Ph.D. degree in electronic and computer engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong in 2020. He is a presently postdoctoral research associate in the State Key Laboratory of Advanced Displays and Optoelectronics Technologies, HKUST. His current research is focused on metal oxide thin-film transistors for displays and sensor interfaces, stenciling technologies for ultra-high-resolution OLED displays, halide perovskite (opto-)electronics, and complex oxide devices for neuromorphic computing. Sunbin has published over 30 peer-reviewed papers and written 1 book chapter. He has also contributed to 8 patents. He was a recipient of distinguished paper award from International Display Manufacturing Conference (IDMC) in 2015 and 2017 and best presentation award from Cross-Strait Postgraduate Workshop on Display Research in 2016 and 2018. His recent work has been nominated as a distinguished paper of SID 2021 Symposium.
